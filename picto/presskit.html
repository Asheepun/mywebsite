Main Developer Dropped Out of College

Picto started as a hobby project by me (Gustav Almstr√∂m), while I was studying at Lund University. I had previously released 3 games on Steam, but none of them had too much financial success. However, when I first implemented the system that let a platforming character jump around inside my phone's camera, I realised that I had found something which really had a lot of potential.


All of my previous projects have been completed with a zero dollar budget, but for this game I decided that I wanted to do it more professionally. I hired the incredibly taltented Comic artist Olof Nilsson, who I found on a Discord server, to draw some art for a demo of the game. I then hand wrote over 200 emails to different video game publishers, all of which turned down the project. This happened in October of 2024, which has been said to be one of the toughest years in the game industry.
